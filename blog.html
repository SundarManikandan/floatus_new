What are the differences between Dry and Wet Float?

  • JUNE 20, 2024

Wet float therapy emphasizes the sensation of floating in warm, buoyant water within a sensory-deprived environment, while dry float therapy offers a weightless experience on a cushioned surface. Individuals may choose between the two based on personal preferences and therapeutic goals.
